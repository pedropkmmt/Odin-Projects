Rice Recipe

Its white fluffy and can be eaten with many options

Rice Recipe

  • Water or vegetable/chicken broth
  • cooking oil or butter
  • salt

Steps

  1. Rinse the rice: Place the rice in a bowl and cover with water. Swirl it around, pour off the cloudy water, and repeat until the water is clear.
  2. Boil: Put the rinsed rice, water, salt, and butter into a pot. Turn the heat to high and bring to a rapid boil.
  3. Simmer: Turn the heat to the lowest setting. Put the lid tightly on the pot and let it cook for exactly 15 minutes.
  4. Rest: Turn off the heat. Leave the lid on and let the rice sit for 10 minutes. This allows steam to finish cooking the grains.
  5. Fluff: Remove the lid and use a fork to gently fluff the rice so the grains separate.
